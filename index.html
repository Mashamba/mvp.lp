For veteran Farmers

Manage your production & collaboration.

Manage farm tasks and volunteers. Scale harvests by creating joint crop plans with neighboring farms. Poll demand from local consumers and commercial purchasers. And more...

All from the convenience of Mashamba's intuitive mobile interface.

for new farmers

Access land & feed communities

Use our Crowdgrow tools to secure and cultivate new land. Easily see where land is available and what other local growers are saying. Select urban and/or rural locations. Join other growers to fill commercial contracts and/or develop your own niche markets. Collaborate with other growers or branch out to new uncultivated areas.

With Mashamba, your horizons and options are boundless.

For Communities

Create hyper-local agronomies.

Would you like to repurpose unused community land into profitable garden space with your neighbors? Do you want to create relationships with the farmers in your community? Are you ready to restore a tangible sense of community?

Mashamba helps you build a local food system... In your community. Join us.

for local food lovers

The freshest local produce available.

Hire a local grower to create your own yardfarm. Support the farmers in your neighborhood. Purchase traditional CSA memberships as well as real-time market offerings sent to your phone or email. Arrange pickups or deliveries to work or home. Plan your diet around seasonally available produce.

All possible with Mashamba on your mobile device.
